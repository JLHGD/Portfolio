About

I graduated in 2023 from Rochtester Institute of Technology with a Bachelor's in Game Design and Development. I work with a variety of game engines like Godot, Unity, and Unreal. In terms of languages, I have experience in Javascript, Java, Python, C#, and C++.

As of 2025, I have completed my associates in biology, moving on to study medical laboratory sciences. I plan on using this knowledge, as well as any knowledge I gain from working in the medical field, to create simulations or otherwise further understanding of autoimmune disorders, such as type 1 diabetes.

Past working on stuff, I like to create and experience many forms of art, whether it be written or painted. I like to mess around in Blender or in a Google Doc when not just playing video games or helping my family.
