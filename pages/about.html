About

I'm currently a 4th year Game Design and Development major at the Rochester Institute of Technology. I work with a variety of game engines like Godot, Unity, and Unreal. In terms of languages, I have experience in Javascript, Java, Python, C#, and C++.

Outside of programming and designing for games, I also make websites, like this one. HTML, CSS, Javascript, and Vue are all tools that I regularly use now, especially when I want to get a quick little personal project done that doesn't require a full game engine.

Past working on stuff, I like to create and experience many forms of art, whether it be written or painted. I like to mess around in Blender or in a Google Doc when not just playing video games or helping my family.
